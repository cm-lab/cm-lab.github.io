    About

    This project is a Master's Thesis conducted by
    Oguzhan Cansever.

    Big Idea: Designing a web-based tool that enables a cross-screen retargeting for digital marketing campaigns. The most important research question is whether applying Lean UX principles allow to identify industry needs and validate Product/Market Fit.


    Campaign Manager: We assume a product as the project outcome that can generate dynamic target URLs for a specific campaign based on users' device (desktop/tablet/phone), channel (website, mobile app).
